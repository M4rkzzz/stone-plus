### 1. StonePlus 品牌资产

本政策适用于 StonePlus 项目及其维护者用于识别官方来源的品牌资产，包括：

- **Stone+**、**StonePlus**、**Stone Plus** 及容易造成混淆的变体；
- Logo、应用图标、安装器图形、官方界面和宣传视觉；
- 官方更新服务、更新地址、发布渠道；
- 官方代码签名证书、签名身份、校验结果和 provenance 标识。

本政策不主张上游 Stone 或任何第三方的名称、商标、图标和素材。相关权利归各自权利人。

### 2. 与源码许可证的关系

StonePlus 自有材料按照仓库 `LICENSE` 中的 **StonePlus Source Available License 1.0**
提供。该许可证只授予查看、审计、学习、有限本地测试、安全研究及向官方渠道提交建议所需的
有限权限，不授权修改、衍生产品、Fork、重新包装、分发、商业/托管运营或 AI 自动改造。

源码可见不构成商标许可。即使品牌文件存在于公开仓库，也不得推定获得名称、Logo、官方视觉、
更新渠道或签名身份的使用权。第三方材料继续适用其原许可证和通知。
官方仓库、维护者账号、证书指纹及 provenance 策略以 `PROJECT_IDENTITY.json` 为准；复制该文件
或伪造相同 remote 地址不构成官方身份。

### 3. 无需许可的真实指称

在不会造成来源、赞助或官方身份混淆的前提下，可以使用纯文字名称进行必要的事实性指称，例如：

- 在评论、教程、新闻、论文、安全报告或版本比较中指称 StonePlus；
- 说明某个问题影响 Stone+；
- 说明提交内容是提供给 StonePlus 项目的建议。

此类使用不得把 StonePlus 名称作为产品、项目、组织、域名、账号或服务的名称，不得使用 Logo、
应用图标或仿制官方视觉，也不得暗示获得官方制作、认证、赞助或背书。

### 4. 禁止的使用

未经 StonePlus 相应权利人事先书面许可，不得：

1. 将 StonePlus 品牌资产用于修改版、Fork、Clone、换标版、重包装版或衍生产品；
2. 只替换名称、Logo、颜色、界面或少量代码后，把基于 StonePlus 的产品宣传为独立原创；
3. 注册或使用容易被误认为官方的域名、社交账号、应用商店名称、组织名或软件包名；
4. 连接、查询、伪装或复用 StonePlus 官方更新服务、发布渠道或下载地址；
5. 使用、复制、模仿或声称拥有 StonePlus 的代码签名证书、签名身份或 provenance；
6. 删除、隐藏或歪曲 LICENSE、NOTICE、来源说明、第三方归属或品牌声明；
7. 利用人工、生成式 AI、脚本或其他自动化手段实施上述行为；
8. 使用 StonePlus 品牌实施欺骗、恶意软件分发或其他违法行为。

更换品牌并不会使一个未经许可的衍生产品变得合规；源码使用权仍由 `LICENSE` 决定。

### 5. 官方构建

只有 StonePlus 项目指定渠道提供、通过官方流程生成并通过签名、校验和及 provenance 验证的构建，
才可称为“StonePlus 官方构建”或“Stone+ 官方版本”。第三方可以真实报告核验结果，但不得伪造、
移植或误导性展示官方身份。

### 6. 权利保留与联系

除本政策明确允许的有限真实指称外，StonePlus 品牌资产的全部权利均予保留。授权申请请通过：

This policy covers source-identifying assets used by the StonePlus project and
its maintainers, including:

- **Stone+**, **StonePlus**, **Stone Plus**, and confusingly similar variants;

- official update services, endpoints, and release channels; and
- official code-signing certificates, signing identities, verification
  results, and provenance indicators.

This policy claims no right in the upstream Stone brand or any third-party
name, mark, icon, or material. Those rights remain with their owners.

StonePlus-owned material is provided under the **StonePlus Source Available

inspection, learning, local evaluation, security research, and submissions to
official channels. It does not permit modification, derivative products,
forks, repackaging, distribution, commercial or hosted operation, or automated
AI transformation.

Source visibility grants no trademark license. The presence of brand files in
a public repository does not imply permission to use a name, logo, official
visual identity, update channel, or signing identity. Third-party material
remains subject to its own licenses and notices.
The canonical repository, maintainer account, certificate fingerprints, and
provenance policy are recorded in `PROJECT_IDENTITY.json`; copying that file or
imitating the remote URL does not establish official identity.

### 3. Permitted truthful reference

You may use a plain-text StonePlus name when reasonably necessary for truthful
commentary, tutorials, news, papers, security reports, issue reports, or
version comparisons, provided that the use creates no confusion about source,
sponsorship, or official status.

Such use must not adopt a StonePlus name as the name of a product, project,
organization, domain, account, package, or service; use the logo, application
icon, or imitated official visual identity; or imply official production,
certification, sponsorship, or endorsement.

### 4. Prohibited uses

Without prior written permission from the applicable StonePlus rightsholder,
you may not:

1. use any StonePlus Brand Asset for a modification, fork, clone, rebranded or
   repackaged build, or derivative product;
2. replace only names, logos, colors, interface elements, or small portions of
   code and present a StonePlus-based product as independently original;
3. register or use a confusing domain, social account, app-store listing,
   organization name, or package name;
4. connect to, query, impersonate, or reuse an official StonePlus updater,
   release channel, or download endpoint;
5. use, copy, imitate, or claim possession of a StonePlus signing certificate,
   signing identity, or provenance;
6. remove, hide, or misrepresent LICENSE, NOTICE, source attribution,
   third-party attribution, or brand notices;
7. use human effort, generative AI, scripts, or other automation to perform a
   prohibited act; or
8. use StonePlus Brand Assets for deception, malware, or unlawful activity.

Changing the branding does not make an unauthorized derivative product
compliant. Source-code permission remains governed by `LICENSE`.

### 5. Official builds

Only builds distributed through channels designated by StonePlus, generated
by its official release process, and verifiable through official signatures,
checksums, and provenance may be described as an “official StonePlus build” or
“official Stone+ release.” Third parties may truthfully report verification
results but may not forge, transplant, or misleadingly display official
identity.

Except for the limited truthful-reference permission above, all rights in the
StonePlus Brand Assets are reserved. Request permission through:
<https://github.com/M4rkzzz/stone-plus>
